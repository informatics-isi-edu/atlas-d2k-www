Project Description

This project leverages a multidisciplinary team with expertise in medicinal chemistry, zebrafish and mouse models of kidney injury, as well as human kidney organoid models, to investigate new therapeutics and pathways that promote healthy repair and improve functional recovery after acute kidney injury.
