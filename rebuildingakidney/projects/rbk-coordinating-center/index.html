Project Description

The Coordinating Center manages activities of the ReBuilding a Kidney Consortium, including research and collaboration opportunities, and facilitates communication of research results, data, and methods within the Consortium and to the community. We created and manage a Data Hub to accelerate the pace of collaboration within the Consortium and disseminate results to the broader research community.
