Bringing data to knowledge (D2K)

During the first year of funding, we are focusing on making this online resource the central hub of data discovery with access to visualizations and analysis of cutting-edge biomedical mouse and human data for the kidney and lower urinary tract regions. Our data respository includes the full contributions of the GUDMAP and ReBuilding A Kidney Consortiums with growing contributions from HuBMAP and KPMP and will soon include more intersections between data, knowledge and analysis tools from all of these consortiums.
