The GenitoUrinary Development Molecular Anatomy Project (GUDMAP) provides data and tools
that facilitate research on the GenitoUrinary (GU) tract for the scientific and medical community.

Browse these shortcuts to GUDMAP's data available in ATLAS-D2K

  • SPECIES include Mouse, Human and Dog

  • Specimen Data Represents the tissue used in experiments and is where imaging data is stored.
    • Immunohistochemistry (IHC)

    • Histology
    • NanoCT
    • Transgenic Fluorescent Imaging (TG)
    • Enzyme Histochemistry
  • OMICS DATA
  • Study data Describes studies of omics data, organized by Experiment and Replicate records.
    • mRNA-Seq
