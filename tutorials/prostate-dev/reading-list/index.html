To learn more about prostate development:

  • History of prostate development research:
    • Timms et al 2008 Differentiation 76:565-77.
  • mRNA markers of developing prostate:
    • Keil et al 2012, Differentiation 84:232-9.
    • Abler et al 2011 Dev Dyn 240:2364-77.
  • Developing prostate and urinary tract microanatomy:
    • Georgas et al 2015 Development 142: 1893–1908.
  • Inductive mechanisms of prostate development and patterning:
    • Thomson 2008 Differentiation 2008 76:587-98.
    • Prins and Putz 2008 Differentiation 76:641-59.
    • Allgeier et al 2010 Dev Dyn. 239: 373–385.
  • Rodent prostate budding and branching morphogenesis:
    • Sugimura et al 1986 Biol Reprod 34:961-71.
    • Lin et al 2003 Toxicol Sci. 76:171-81.
    • Timms and Hofkamp 2011 Differentiation 82:173-83.
